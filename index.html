The Lab

The Lab is free to use and we're open 11am-5pm, Monday to Friday.

We're on the bottom floor at The Old BakeHouse, 18A Ivy Lane, Canterbury, Kent, CT1 1TU. We’re a bit tucked away, so look out for lots of glass and a sign for Red Bullet.

You're welcome to drop in any time, but we love to know when people are coming so we can pop the coffee on and stock the biscuit tin. Drop us a line

Donate
